Ceremony & Reception

Our ceremony will take place outdoors amongst the grapevines at Wiens Family Cellars in Temecula, CA. The ceremony will begin at 4:30pm. Please, plan accordingly to allow enough time for a prompt arrival. The Temecula Valley Balloon and Wine Festival is the same weekend, so there may be more traffic than usual. The attire is semi-formal/dressy casual; basically, whatever makes you look spiffy and feel comfortable!

Cocktail hour and reception will follow at the same venue until 10:00pm. The reception will be indoors with plenty of food, drinks, and dancing.
